## **Course Summary**

### Total Content: 310 Slides
- **10 Opening Power Charts** (10 slides - 1 per week)
- **30% Theory & Fundamentals** (93 slides)
- **35% Technical Implementation** (108 slides)
- **25% Design Integration** (78 slides)
- **10% Advanced Topics** (31 slides)

### Learning Progression:
1. **Foundation** → Understanding dual pipelines
2. **Empathize** → Clustering & NLP for user understanding
3. **Define** → Classification & Topic Modeling for problems
4. **Ideate** → Generation & Feature Analysis for solutions
5. **Prototype** → Structured generation & validation
6. **Test** → Evaluation & ethical deployment

### Key Outcomes:
- Master 10 core ML/AI techniques
- Integrate ML with Design Thinking
- Build production-ready innovation pipelines
- Deploy ethically and responsibly
- Create compound innovation value
